This is the summarized life of Elon Musk

  • Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • At age 10, with his first computer, a Commodore VIC-20, he began to learn programming on his own. Two years later, he sold his first video game.
  • At 17, after graduating from high school in Pretoria, he decided to leave South Africa.
  • In 1992, thanks to a scholarship, he was able to study Business Administration and Physics at the University of Pennsylvania.
  • He received his degree from the Wharton School and stayed at the university for another year to complete his second degree in physics. After obtaining his degrees, he decided to dedicate himself to the Internet, renewable energy and the conquest of space.
  • He founded Zip in 1995. In 1999 success was latent, he managed a total of 200 websites, something that led him to sell it for 300 million dollars to AltaVista
  • In March 1999 Elon Musk, Ho, Harris Fricker, and Cristopher Payne founded X.com as a financial startup. Some time later it would be called PayPal and it would be bought by Ebay for 1500 million dollars.
  • In June 2002, Musk founded his third company, Space Exploration Technologies. SpaceX is dedicated to developing and producing space shuttles, with an emphasis on cost reduction and high reliability. On December 23, 2008 SpaceX signed a contract of 1.6 billion dollars with NASA
  • In April 2004 Elon Musk decided to invest $ 6.3 million in Tesla Motors. By 2012, 2,100 Tesla Roadsters were sold in various countries. In 2015 the Tesla Model X was launched, designed to travel all types of terrain.
  • Musk is a co-founder of SolarCity with his cousin Lyndon Rive. In addition, the South African chairs the company's board of directors as the main investor in the company. The company's role is to create photovoltaic products and provide related services. In 2011, it became the number 1 company in the United States to provide solar energy systems in order to make the most of this little-used energy.
  • Neuralink is a nanobiotechnology company founded by Musk that aims to integrate the human brain with artificial intelligence. The company was founded in 2016 and became known to the general public for the first time in March 2017.
  • OpenAI is a non-profit artificial intelligence (AI) research company that aims to promote and develop friendly artificial intelligence in such a way that it benefits humanity as a whole.
